URLhaus threat intelligence (optional)

URLhaus (by abuse.ch) is CookieSpy's strongest malware-domain signal. As of 2024 it requires a free Auth-Key. Without one, CookieSpy still works fully and keeps scoring domains with the keyless DNS checks — URLhaus simply adds deeper malware coverage on top.

The key is free and personal to you: sign in at auth.abuse.ch with a Google/GitHub/X/LinkedIn account, create an Auth-Key under the "Optional" section, and paste it below — about two minutes.

CookieSpy ships with no shared key — each user brings their own. That keeps you a normal, individual user under abuse.ch's fair-use terms. Your key is stored only on this device and is sent only to abuse.ch.

Verifying the block UI

To see the auto-block and timed-release UI without hunting for a real malicious site, visit testsafebrowsing.appspot.com. CookieSpy treats that host as a built-in self-test entry and scores it 100, so it will appear blocked in the popup with the full allow menu.
